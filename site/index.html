The point

A sales CRM remembers what helps it sell. Liaison should remember what helps you show up.

01

A person is not a lead

Relationships do not belong in a funnel. The model keeps intent, evidence and private judgement separate.

02

Frequency is not closeness

Message count cannot prove trust, affection or importance. Liaison records facts and explains review reasons.

03

Unknown means unknown

A blank allergy, accessibility or preference field is never treated as “none”. Uncertainty stays visible.

04

Your files remain yours

Human-scale records use Markdown and YAML. Indexes, search and graph layouts can be rebuilt.

Product direction

Less remembering to remember

The planned loop is small: capture the useful detail, find it before it matters, understand why something needs attention and follow through without a guilt queue.

  1. 1

    Capture

    Record a note, preference, promise, date or source while the context is fresh.

  2. 2

    Recall

    Bring the right context back by person, event, organisation, commitment or purpose.

  3. 3

    Prepare

    Build a source-linked briefing that shows what is known, stale, missing or private.

  4. 4

    Follow through

    Keep the promise visible. Snooze, pause or stop when that is the right boundary.

Local authority

Open the folder. Read your records. Keep them if the app disappears.

Canonical records live in an ordinary directory. Liaison can build search indexes, graphs and thumbnails around them, but those projections never become the only copy.

Straight answers

Before you clone it

Can I download Liaison RM today?

Not as a supported product. The repository is pre-alpha and has no signed public release. Developers can build the current CLI and desktop alpha from source.

Does it need an Electric Town account?

No. The local workspace is the authority. A hosted account or relationship database is not required.

Is this a sales CRM?

No. Liaison does not treat people as leads or infer closeness from message volume. It separates relationship intent, recorded evidence, maintenance status and purpose-specific readiness.

Will it send my private notes to an AI service?

No AI provider receives personal data without an explicit provider, purpose, scope and expiry grant. Local-model support is planned; AI output remains untrusted input.

Are Irish, Japanese and Brazilian Portuguese available?

Not as released locales. The repository has catalog architecture and draft fixtures, but no non-source locale has the named human review and accessibility evidence required for release.
